## Analysis

### 1. What is the "local baseline"?

De lokale rijschoolmarkt in Tiel/Betuwe is **zwak qua websites**:
- Allemaal WordPress-templates (Divi, Elementor, Flatsome) — geen enkele custom site
- Minimale trust signals: geen enkele lokale concurrent toont CBR-slagingspercentages
- Reviews amper zichtbaar: alleen Rijschool de Betuwe heeft reviews prominent staan
- Prijzen vaak verstopt achter een klik of helemaal afwezig
- Veel stockfoto's, weinig echte studenten/instructeursfoto's
- CTA's zijn generiek ("informatie aanvragen", "proefles aanvragen") zonder urgentie
- WhatsApp-contact wisselend aanwezig (terwijl de doelgroep dit verwacht)

**De Betuwe (#2) is de enige serieuze lokale concurrent** — 35+ jaar, echte foto's, zichtbare prijzen, 9.4+ rating, breed aanbod. De rest scoort matig tot slecht.

**Dit betekent: een professionele, moderne site met CBR-stats en echte geslaagdenfoto's springt er direct uit in deze markt.**

### 2. What do the national leaders do differently?

- **Social proof op schaal**: GLOW heeft 240+ reviews met 5.0/5, Nationale Rijschool 1.662+ reviews. Lokale scholen tonen weinig of niks
- **Garanties**: Nationale Rijschool biedt herexamengarantie (€350 waarde), Noord biedt 100% rijbewijsgarantie. Geen enkele lokale school biedt garanties
- **Meerdere contactkanalen**: Telefoon + WhatsApp + formulier + live chat. Lokaal is het vaak alleen telefoon + formulier
- **Pricing transparency**: GLOW en Noord tonen duidelijke pakketten met prijzen. Meteoor toont zelfs proefles-prijs (€40)
- **Professionele badges/certificeringen**: GLOW toont CBR + BOVAG + IBKI + 2toDrive. Lokaal ontbreken deze vaak
- **Conversion-optimized CTAs**: Meerdere formulieren per pagina, postcode-zoekers, direct-aanmelden buttons. Lokaal: 1 formulier ergens onderaan
- **Echte foto's**: Zowel GLOW als Nationale Rijschool hebben echte studentfoto's. GLOW combineert dit met professionele shots

### 3. What mistakes do the worst competitors make?

1. **Geen reviews tonen** (Jaddi, Van Thiel, Rijschool Tiel) — in een markt waar ouders betalen en vergelijken, is dit fataal
2. **Prijzen volledig verbergen** (Jaddi, Van Thiel, Xplore) — Nederlandse consumenten verwachten transparantie, verborgen prijzen wekken wantrouwen
3. **Stockfoto's gebruiken** (Jaddi, Van Thiel, Meteoor, Rijschool Tiel) — in een persoonlijke dienst als rijles is authenticiteit cruciaal
4. **Geen CBR-slagingspercentages tonen** — geen enkele concurrent (lokaal of nationaal!) laat dit prominent zien. Dit is de grootste gemiste kans in de hele sector
5. **Generieke WordPress-templates zonder persoonlijkheid** — de meeste sites zijn inwisselbaar, geen enkele weerspiegelt de persoonlijkheid van de instructeur
6. **Zwakke mobiele ervaring** — zware Elementor/Divi-code, trage laadtijden, terwijl 70%+ van rijschool-zoekers mobiel is
7. **Geen WhatsApp-optie** (Van Thiel, enkele anderen) — de primaire doelgroep (16-25 jaar) communiceert via WhatsApp/DM
8. **Te breed claimen zonder bewijs** — Speed claimt "beste rijschool van Nederland" zonder enige onderbouwing

## Deep Design Analysis — Top 3

### #1 Nationale Rijschool (9/10)

**Section order:** Promo bar → Hero ("Rijbewijs halen. Later betalen.") → Postcode planner → 3 stat cards (45.824 geslaagden, slagingspercentage, korting) → Spoedcursus → Testimonial carousel → 3 content blocks → Planner repeat → Benefits → Recruitment → Support options → Footer

**Typography:** Modern sans-serif stack. Bold headings ~48-56px. Regular body. ALL CAPS used sparingly (promo banner only). Clean hierarchy.

**Colors:** Green primary (trust/growth), warm gold/yellow accent (bee brand element 🐝), white backgrounds, dark charcoal text. High contrast CTA buttons.

**Layout patterns:**
- Full-bleed hero with edge-to-edge imagery
- 3-column stat cards with generous whitespace
- Testimonial carousel with real student photos
- Postcode-entry form repeated 3x on page (conversion-obsessed)
- Overlapping promo banner on hero

**Spacing:** Generous around hero and major blocks. Tighter in nav. Varied per section — not uniform.

**Decorative:** Emoji usage in content (🐝✅🚀🇳🇱💰⭐️🎉). Rounded corners on cards/buttons. Minimal SVG decoration.

**Animation:** Carousel auto-advance. Smooth button transitions. No heavy scroll animations.

**Trust signals:** Rating displayed as large text ("Uitstekend" 4.8/5 + 1.662+ reviews). Stat cards with big numbers. Named student testimonials. Guarantee badge (herexamengarantie €350).

**CTA buttons:** Solid fill, contrasting colors, rounded corners. "Boek nu je proefles" / "Toon beschikbaarheid" as primary text.

**Why it works (user said "AI but done super well"):** Scale-based trust (huge numbers), payment flexibility as hero hook, conversion-optimized with multiple planners, clean information hierarchy.

**Why it works (user said "looks professional"):** SVG wave dividers add visual sophistication. Gradient CTAs stand out. Google badge immediately establishes trust. 20+ real student photos build authenticity. Clean pricing grid.

## Design Decisions Summary

### 1. Top 3-5 Design Decisions to Incorporate

1. **SVG wave section dividers** (GLOW) — cheap way to add visual sophistication over flat section transitions. Use between hero and first content block, and between key section transitions
2. **Gradient CTA buttons** (GLOW: orange→red primary, blue secondary) — dual gradients with 300ms hover transitions outperform flat buttons visually. Nationale Rijschool also uses high-contrast solid CTAs
3. **Stat row with big numbers** (De Betuwe: "35+ Jaar | 5000+ Uren | 1000+ Geslaagden | 9.4+ Cijfer") — large typography for 3-4 key metrics immediately after hero. For Goed op Weg: CBR slagingspercentages here
4. **Real student photo grid** (GLOW: 20+ photos) — dedicated gallery section with authentic celebration photos. Goed op Weg already has these from Facebook. This is the strongest differentiator vs all competitors
5. **Google/review badge in hero** (GLOW: 5.0/5 badge right under headline) — immediate trust signal before any scrolling. For Goed op Weg: 15 reviews at 5 sterren

### 2. CSS Layout Patterns That Make Them Professional

| Pattern | Used by | Implementation |
|---|---|---|
| Full-bleed hero with dark overlay | All 3 | `background-size: cover` + `rgba()` overlay |
| SVG wave dividers between sections | GLOW | `background-image: url(data:image/svg+xml;base64...)` at `100% 60px`, flip with `transform: scale(-1,1)` |
| Pill-shaped buttons | De Betuwe | `border-radius: 9999px` |
| Card shadows | GLOW | `box-shadow: 0px 0px 50px 0px rgba(24,24,24,0.08)` |
| Gradient buttons | GLOW | `linear-gradient(90deg, #start, #end)` with hover state change |
| Overlapping ribbon/badge | GLOW | `transform: translate(-40px,10px) rotate(-45deg)` on featured element |
| Alternating 2-col layouts | De Betuwe, GLOW | Image-left/text-right, then text-left/image-right per section |
| Multi-hero (per service) | De Betuwe | Full-bleed hero per service type — uncommon, distinctive |
| Varying section backgrounds | GLOW | Alternating `#fff` / `#f7f7f7` / `#ddf6ff` / gradient between sections |
| Generous, non-uniform padding | All 3 | `40-80px` section padding, NOT the same for every section |

**Industry nav vocabulary insights:**

- "Tarieven" or "Prijslijst" instead of "Prijzen" — more professional
- "Vraag & antwoord" preferred over "FAQ" by GLOW
- Primary CTA as nav button: "Boek een proefles" / "Aanmelden proefles"
- "Inschrijven" is a common action term (not just "Contact")
- Service pages named by license type: "Auto Opleiding", "Motor Opleiding", "Bromfiets Opleiding"

### 4. What ALL Competitors Do the Same (= Differentiation Opportunities)

1. **Nobody shows CBR slagingspercentages prominently** — not a single competitor (local or national) puts actual CBR exam pass rates in their hero or stat row. Goed op Weg's 91% motor, 90% bromfiets rates would be UNIQUE
2. **Everyone uses WordPress** — all 10 competitors are on WordPress (Elementor, Divi, Flatsome, Uncode). An Astro static site will be dramatically faster (sub-second load times vs 3-5 second WP averages)
3. **Generic celebration of passing** — all show "geslaagd!" posts but none with the energetic Gen-Z voice Goed op Weg uses. The brand personality is a differentiator
4. **No dagcursus bromfiets as standalone product** — competitors list bromfiets as a service, but nobody markets the dagcursus (one-day course) as a hero product with its own page/CTA
5. **No WhatsApp as primary CTA** — most offer WhatsApp as secondary/tertiary. For the 16-25 demographic, making WhatsApp the PRIMARY contact method (above phone) would match user behavior
6. **Flat section transitions everywhere** — only GLOW uses SVG wave dividers. Most use hard cuts between sections. Visual polish opportunity
7. **No instructor personality in the design** — even De Betuwe with instructor profiles treats them as generic cards. Nobody's site reflects the instructor's actual voice/energy in the design language
